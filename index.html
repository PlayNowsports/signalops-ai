ONE-PAGE OPERATING GUIDE

Weekly renewal and expansion triage

1ExportRevOps exports the current Salesforce opportunity book as XLSX or CSV. Keep the original field names.
2Upload and validateThe file stays in the browser. Blocking schema issues stop the run; row-level contradictions become explicit findings.
3Review the queueRevOps reviews Critical and High accounts, evidence, and proposed owner/action. No score should be used without its evidence.
4Route and exportExport the weekly CSV. CSM owns adoption/health actions, AE owns commercial actions, and leadership resolves coverage or exception decisions.
5Learn deliberatelyChange rules through Rule Memory, record the reason, export the rule pack, and compare outcomes next quarter.
